About

This calculator was made by Plansup in collaboration with Saxion. With the help of this tool, companies have an opportunity to calculate their biodiversity footprint online. The tool is based on dose response relationships of two pressure factors from the GLOBIO methodology and therefore it gives only an indication of the generic impact on biodiversity. No rights can be derived from the results obtained. Plansup and Saxion do not accept any liability for decisions taken based on results obtained.

Collaboration with Saxion

Saxion University of Applied Sciences gave an opportunity to students to practice and improve their skills by working in teams on different projects. In turn, these projects are carried out in collaboration with companies. This type of interaction is incredibly useful, as everyone benefits. This calculator is the result of a collaboration between Saxion University students and Plansup.

Open source

The open source BF calculator was developed by Plansup in collaboration with Wageningen Environmental Research. The research of the underlying methodology and the development of the calculator was carried out with public money and therefore the calculator is fully accessible and free of charge. It is expected that the tool will be improved in the future on the basis of additional research. If you adjust the tool functionality, this must be reported when results are published. Suggestions for improvement / adaptation of the tool can be sent to: plansup.consult@gmail.com.

Get in contact

In case you have questions about the tool or would like to get training data, please write an e-mail to Wilbert van Rooij via plansup.consult@gmail.com and give a short description for what purpose you will use the tool. You can use the same address in case you would like to get assistance with the inventory of input data, or would like to get a more comprehensive analysis based on the full BF method. Support can also be provided for an analysis of potential biodiversity friendly measures.
